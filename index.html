about

  • DJ Silverfox

    DJ Silverfox has been involved in the music and nightlife industry for over twenty years. From London, to Ibiza, Tokyo, he has developed deep relationship with music and entertainment. He has a true passion for music and it shows through every time he is behind the decks. He is unquestionably the top after-house DK in the Roppongi area of Tokyo. In 2008, after years of ruling the after-hours party scene, DJ Silverfox reached into his extensive network of Dj’s and established BeatForce to spread the Tokyo underground sound around the world.
